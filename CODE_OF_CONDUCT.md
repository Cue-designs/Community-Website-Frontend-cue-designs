# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to making the DevByte Community a welcoming space for everyone. Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Use inclusive language.
- Provide helpful feedback.
- Respect different viewpoints.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or discrimination.
- Offensive comments or imagery.
- Trolling or personal attacks.
- Disrupting discussions or events.

## Enforcement
Violations may result in temporary or permanent removal from the community. Reports can be sent confidentially to the DevByte team.

## Attribution
This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) version 2.1.
